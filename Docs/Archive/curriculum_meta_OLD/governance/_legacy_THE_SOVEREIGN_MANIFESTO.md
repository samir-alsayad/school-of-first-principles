# The Sovereign Manifesto: The Grand Map of Interests
**Status**: Living Document
**Goal**: To organize the "Meta Project" -> A system that governs all creative and technical pursuits of the Sovereign.

## 1. The Core Philosophy
*   **The Meta Project**: Collect all data (logs, works, ideas), organize it, and create agents to assist in every domain.
*   **Data Sovereignty**: "The Big Data Recycling". Recapturing ownership of all personal data (Google, Maps, Email) into a structure owned by the User.
*   **Local & Open**: Zero reliance on walled gardens (Apple, Google). Transition to Open Source & Local Tech.
