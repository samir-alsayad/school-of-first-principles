## 3. The Anatomy of a "Unit"
Whether it's a Campaign or a Module, every **Unit** has the same internal organs (The Fractal).

*   **Foundry**: The Design Studio. Where we think, spec, and draft.
    *   *Output*: `intent.md`, `spec.md`, `casting_guide.md`.
*   **Lab**: The Workshop. Where we play, break things, and experiment.
    *   *Output*: `notebook.md`, `experiments/`.
*   **Codex**: The Knowledge Base. The Surveyor's output.
    *   *Output*: `codex.md`.

## 4. The Workflow Vocabulary
*   **The Surveyor**: I list what exists.
*   **The Analyst**: I explain what it means.
*   **The Initiate**: I learn how to do it in the Lab.
*   **The Architect**: I design the solution in the Foundry.
*   **The Pour**: I execute the "Casting Guide" to move code from the School to the Project.
